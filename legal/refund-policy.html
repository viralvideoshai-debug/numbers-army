Refund Policy

App: Numbers Army  ·  Effective date: July 5, 2026

1. Purchases through Google Play

All in-app purchases made in the Android app are processed by Google Play. Refunds follow Google Play's refund policy: you can request a refund from Google within 48 hours of purchase directly from your order history; after 48 hours, refund requests are reviewed by the developer.

2. Requesting a refund from us

Email viralvideoshai@gmail.com with your Google Play order ID (starts with "GPA."), the date of purchase, and the reason. We respond within 5 business days. We approve refunds for: duplicate charges, purchases that failed to deliver the item, accidental purchases reported promptly, and unauthorized purchases confirmed by Google.

3. What is not refundable

Virtual currency or items that have already been spent or consumed in-game, and purchases older than 30 days, are generally not refundable except where required by law.

4. Consumer law

Nothing in this policy limits rights you have under applicable consumer protection law in your country.
