Cookie Policy

Website / Web game: Numbers Army  ·  Effective date: July 5, 2026

1. What we use

The web version of Numbers Army does not use tracking cookies of its own. It uses localStorage (a browser storage technology similar to cookies) strictly to save your game progress on your device: coins, levels, upgrades, sound settings, and daily-bonus dates. This data is functional, never sent to us, and never used for advertising by us.

2. Third-party cookies

If the web version includes advertising or analytics (e.g., Google AdSense/AdMob, Google Analytics), those providers may set cookies or use similar identifiers for ad delivery, frequency capping, and measurement. Where legally required, a consent banner will ask your permission before such cookies are set.

3. Managing storage

You can clear the game's localStorage via your browser settings (Site settings → Storage → Clear) or with the in-game "Reset save" button. Blocking all storage will make the game unable to save progress but it will still run.
