How to Delete Your Data

App: Numbers Army: Crowd Run 3D  ·  Developer: SocialBucket Studios (Hemant Kumar)  ·  Effective date: July 5, 2026

What data the app holds

Numbers Army stores your game progress (coins, level, upgrades, skins, daily-streak dates, sound and tutorial settings) only on your own device. It also uses Google AdMob (advertising) and Google Firebase Analytics, which collect a device advertising ID and anonymous app-usage events. The developer does not run any server and holds no personal account data about you.

Option 1 — Delete your in-game data (instant)

  1. Open Numbers Army.
  2. On the main menu, tap “↺ Reset save”.
  3. Confirm. This permanently erases all game progress stored on your device.

Alternatively, on Android go to Settings → Apps → Numbers Army → Storage → Clear data, which removes everything the app has saved.

Option 2 — Reset advertising / analytics identifiers

To clear the advertising ID used by ads and analytics: on Android go to Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID (or “Reset advertising ID”). You can also opt out of personalized ads there.

Option 3 — Request deletion by email

To request deletion of any remaining data associated with your device, email viralvideoshai@gmail.com with the subject line “Data deletion request — Numbers Army”. We will action the request and respond within 30 days. Because we operate no user accounts, please include the approximate date you played and your device type so we can assist with the relevant ad/analytics provider.

What is deleted vs kept

Deleted: all on-device game progress and settings; advertising ID on reset. Kept: aggregated, anonymized analytics that cannot be tied back to you may be retained by Google per their policies. We keep no personally identifying account data because none is collected.
